# Side Pots

A side pot calculator.

## How to install it on your phone

1. Open this link in your phone browser: **https://cryptostain.github.io/jjpoker/**
2. Open the browser menu.
   * iPhone (Safari): tap the **Share** button, then **Add to Home Screen**.
   * Android (Chrome): tap the **⋮** menu, then **Add to Home screen** (or **Install app**).
3. Tap **Add**. An icon appears on your home screen.

That is it. Tap the icon any time to open it. It works offline, so you do not need internet at the table.

## How the calculator works

The whole app is one page you scroll. A keypad sits at the bottom for typing numbers.

### 1. Set the blinds

Three tiles at the top: small blind, big blind, ante. Tap one and type the amount.

### 2. Enter what each player put in

Tap a player row, then type how much they bet. Useful keys:

* **Call** fills in the amount needed to match the biggest bet, then jumps to the next player.
* **Fold** marks a player as folded. Their chips stay in the pot, but they cannot win it.
* **All in** marks a player as having no chips left.
* **Clear** wipes that player entry.

Tap **+ Flop** to move to the next round of betting.

### 3. Pick the winner

Each pot shows its size and who is in it. Tap a name to mark the winner. Tap two names for a split.

### 4. See the payout

The app shows how much each winner takes and which pot it came from. If a split does not come out to whole chips, it flags the odd chip so you settle it at the table.

Tap **New hand** to clear everything and start fresh. Players and blind amounts stay.

## What it follows

* The math cares only about the **total** each player put in, not the order or the round.
* Chips from folded players stay in the pot. They just cannot win it.
* If one player bet more than anyone else could match, the extra goes back to them instead of into a pot.
* The app does not track stacks or seats, so you tap **All in** and **Fold** yourself.
